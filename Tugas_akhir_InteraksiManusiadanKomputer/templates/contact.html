Who We Are

LEAFORA began as a small passion project among botanical enthusiasts who shared a common goal: making high-quality greenery accessible to everyone. We noticed that many people wanted to bring nature indoors but didn't know where to start or how to find healthy, sustainable plants.

Today, LEAFORA has grown into a trusted marketplace for plant lovers. We bridge the gap between expert nurseries and your doorstep, ensuring that the beauty of the natural world is never more than a click away.
